## Cluster rationale

- Three different brands ✔ (Therm-a-Rest, NEMO, Sea to Summit); same product type (insulated
  ultralight inflatable pad, Regular); one category (sleep-systems).

  exactly these products.

  and **no money-page roundup** — this is the first sleep-systems roundup, and the existing guide
