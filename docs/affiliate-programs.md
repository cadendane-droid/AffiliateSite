# Affiliate Program Signup Plan

The master list of programs covering not just today's 9 products but the realistic universe of
gear HeavyHiker will ever review across its nine categories. 01-research checks this file
before flagging "program needed" issues. Update `status` as applications progress.

Strategy in one line: **one network (AvantLink) covers ~70% of the niche including nearly every
mainstream and cottage brand we'll review; Amazon is the completeness layer and deliberately
waits; everything else is applied to on demand.**

|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **AvantLink account** — avantlink.com/affiliates | — | free; prerequisite for everything below | the outdoor industry's network |

Applying to merchants inside AvantLink is a short per-merchant form once the account exists.
Approvals want a live site with real content — we have one. If rejected, publish 2–3 more weeks
of daily articles and reapply; rejections are not permanent.

**Bonus:** AvantLink merchant approval unlocks **product datafeeds with licensed product
images** — the approved source that fills the 7 missing product photos (see image-policy.md).

## Tier 2 — apply on demand (when an article features them; most are one-click-ish inside AvantLink)

- **Cottage brands with programs:** Gossamer Gear, Tarptent, Six Moon Designs, Enlightened
  Equipment, ULA Equipment, Hyperlite Mountain Gear, Outdoor Vitals, Katabatic Gear
- **Retail alternates:** CampSaver, Outdoor Gear Exchange (both AvantLink) — price/stock backups
- **Navigation/safety direct:** Garmin (inReach) if REI coverage proves insufficient

Rule for agents: a research brief that features a product with no live program gets a
`PENDING_AFFILIATE_ID` registry entry + a GitHub issue naming the program to join — publishing
never waits on monetization.

## Tier 3 — Amazon Associates: WAIT (deliberately)

Amazon closes new accounts that don't produce **3 qualifying sales within 180 days** of
signup. You can reapply later with a new ID, but the old ID is never reinstated — and our
registry makes the tag swap trivial, so there is zero benefit to applying early.

- **When to apply:** once the site has steady organic/AI-referral traffic — a practical
  threshold is ~50–100 sessions/day on money pages, or the first month PostHog shows 100+
  affiliate clicks. At that point 3 sales in 180 days is near-certain.
- **What it's for:** the completeness layer — budget/import gear no retailer program carries
  (BRS-3000T, 3F UL Gear Lanshan, Naturehike), plus small accessories. Rates are the lowest of
  the set (~3–4% outdoors), so it's coverage, not the money-maker.
- **Until then:** those registry entries keep their manufacturer `fallbackUrl` — articles ship
  and rank; monetization arrives with a one-line registry edit.
